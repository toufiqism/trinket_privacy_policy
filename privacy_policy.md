# Privacy Policy — Trinket

**Last updated:** 29 June 2026

Trinket ("the app") is a fully offline app. This policy explains exactly what
the app does and does not do with your data.

## Summary

Trinket does **not** collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There
is no account, no login, no server, no analytics, and no advertising. Everything
the app does happens **on your device**.

## Data the app uses (all on-device)

- **Camera.** The app uses your camera so you can photograph an object (Object
  mode) or yourself (Character mode). The camera is only active while you are on
  a capture screen.
- **Captured photos.**
  - *Object mode:* the photo you capture is processed on-device into a stylized
    item image and stored **locally on your device** so saved items survive an
    app restart. It is never uploaded.
  - *Character mode:* the selfie is used only to sample colors and expression
    on-device, and is then **deleted immediately**. It is never stored and never
    uploaded.
- **On-device machine learning.** Object recognition, face detection, and pose
  detection run entirely on your device using bundled / on-device Google ML Kit
  models. No image or detection result leaves the device.
- **Local storage.** Saved items and character sheets are stored locally on your
  device (app preferences + the app's private documents folder). They are
  removed when you delete them in the app or uninstall the app.
- **Gallery (optional).** If you choose "save to gallery", the app writes a
  rendered card image to your device's photo gallery, with your permission.
- **Share (optional).** If you choose "share", the app hands a rendered card
  image to your device's system share sheet. Where it goes from there is
  controlled by you and the app you pick to share with.

## Data the app does NOT collect

- No name, email, phone number, or account of any kind.
- No location.
- No contacts, SMS, call logs, or microphone.
- No advertising identifiers.
- No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting.
- No data transmitted to us or any third party — the app makes no network
  requests for your data.

## Network use

Trinket runs offline. The only possible network activity is a **one-time
download of an on-device ML Kit model** (Subject Segmentation) the first time
you stylize an object, performed by Google Play services. This download contains
no personal data of yours; it only fetches the model. After that the app works
fully offline.

## Permissions

- **Camera** — to capture photos in the app. Required for the core feature.
- **Photos / media (storage)** — only to save a rendered card image to your
  gallery if you ask it to.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including
children. It is suitable for general audiences (ages 13+).

## Data retention and deletion

All app data lives on your device. To delete it: remove individual items/sheets
in the app, clear the app's storage in Android settings, or uninstall the app.
Because there is no account and nothing is uploaded, there is no server-side data
to delete.

## Account deletion

The app has no accounts, so there is no account to delete. Uninstalling the app
removes all app data from your device.

## Third parties

Trinket does not share data with third parties. The app uses Google ML Kit
on-device models bundled in or fetched by Google Play services; these run locally
and do not receive your personal data.

## International transfers

No data is transferred off your device, so there are no international data
transfers.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the
updated policy will be published at the same location.
